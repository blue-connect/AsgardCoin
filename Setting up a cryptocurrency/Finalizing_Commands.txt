




Finalizing commands 


Line for printing genesis block:
./Turtlecoind —print-genesis-tx

Line for opening wallet:
./zedwallet —scan-coinbase-transactions

Line for running daemon:
./TurtleCoind
Or in your case most likely 
./MMPLcoind

Line for running miner:
./miner


Lines for compiling the cloned repo:
Cd MMPLcoin
Mkdir build 
Cd build 
Cmake ..
Make 

The above lines are for both local and non local machines
